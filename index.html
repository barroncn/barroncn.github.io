ABOUT ME


I recently graduated from UNC Charlotte with a degree in Mathematics, a minor in Chemistry, and a concentration in Actuarial Science. As I trudged through an internship with a life insurance company, I realized I was on the wrong career path.

I want to utilize my creativity and problem solving skills. I want a career that is challenging and dynamic. After talking to a few different people and doing lots of research, I decided to learn how to code!

UNCC's Coding Bootcamp has given me a solid foundation for a career in Software Development. Among other things, I've gotten extensive practice with Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, MySQL and MongoDB, as well as exposure to pair programming and working in a team environment. Every project I've worked on has reaffirmed my decision to be in this field and has fueled my excitement for the future.

When I'm not practicing coding or knee deep in documentation, you can find me outside. I love hiking, kayaking, and travelling to new places.
